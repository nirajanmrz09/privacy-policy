Introduction

Welcome to Plant Care, a mobile application developed and operated by Amisub Solutions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application on iOS or Android devices.

By downloading and using Plant Care, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you disagree with any part, please discontinue use of the application.

ℹ️

This policy applies to all users worldwide. Our servers and third-party services may be located outside your country of residence.

🗂️

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account credentials – name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile picture – if you choose to upload one from your device gallery.
  • Plant images – photos you capture or select for AI-powered identification and diagnosis.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device location – used only to fetch real-time local weather data for gardening advice.
  • Scan history – plant identification records stored to your account in the cloud.
  • Usage data – app interactions and feature usage to improve performance.
  • Device information – operating system, device model, and app version.

Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In – if you use this, we receive your public profile (name, email, avatar).
  • Subscription data – purchase status and tier from RevenueCat's payment platform.
⚙️

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your user account across devices.
  • To provide AI-powered plant identification and health diagnosis via Google Gemini.
  • To display personalised daily plant care tips from our Firestore database.
  • To fetch real-time local weather data and provide gardening maintenance alerts.
  • To manage your Pro subscription and unlock premium features.
  • To sync your entire plant scan history across all your devices.
  • To send optional daily watering reminders (push notifications).
  • To serve advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob.
  • To monitor app stability, diagnose crashes, and improve performance.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Legal Requirements – if required by law, court order, or enforceable governmental request.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, which provides industry-standard encryption both at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL).

Plant images submitted for AI analysis are processed by the Google Gemini API and are not permanently stored on our servers after the identification session ends.

⚠️

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we implement best-practice security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
